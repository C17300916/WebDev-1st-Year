What is Game of Thrones?

Some of you might be wondering; "Well what is Game of Thrones?". Game of Thrones is an award winning HBO television series created by David Benioff and D.B.Weiss. The series is based on the series of fantasy novels, A Song of Ice and Fire, written by George R.R. Martin. It is critically renowned world wide with 38 Primetime Emmy Awards for its acting, complex plots and witty characters. Currently there has been 7 seasons and the 8th and final season will premiere in either 2018/19. It is an international phenomenon with millions of viewers from accross the globe! For example the season finale of season 7 had an outstanding 16.5 million viewers! It is clearly one of the most popular and arguably one of best shows of this generation!
