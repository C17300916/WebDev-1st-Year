Characters in Game of Thrones

Game of Thrones is filled with interesting and exciting characters throughout. They range from the meanest, most horrid people you would ever meet to a character that you just cant help root and cheer for. For the majority of the show we follow three main plot points and from that we meet a variety of different people. In the first season you will meet the entire Stark family, the savages of the east(Dorthraki) and all of the people involved in the throne. Below is a brief description of some of the characters that you will meet in the first season.

Eddard Stark

Lord Eddard (Ned) Stark is the Lord of Winterfell and one of the main characters in the show. He is known for his love of his family, loyalty and honour. He has 4 sons (including a bastard)and 2 daughters. They are Rob, Bran, Rickon, Jon Snow(bastard), Arya and Sansa. Each of the children are also main characters and you will get to know them throughout.

Catelyn Stark

Catelyn Stark is Eddard's wife but was originally a Tully and daughter to Hoster Tully. She is a tough and strong woman with a great love for her children.

Robert Baratheon

King Robert Baratheon is the ruler of the Seven Kingdom. Often considred incompetent for his constant drinking and lack of care. He is best friends with Eddard Stark.

Jaime Lannister

Jaime Lannister is the heir to the Lannisters and also known as the Kingslayer for killing the previous King while he was his Hand.

Tywin Lannister

Tywin Lannister is head of House Lannister. He is a ruthless and cunning leader. He also is the leader of the Lannister army and considered to be one of the greatest war strategist.

Cersei Lannister

Cersei Lannister is the Queen of the Seven Kingdoms. She is mother to the heirs of the throne, Joffery, Myrcella and Tommen. She is known to be cunning and manipulative like her father.

Tyrion Lannister

Tyrion is brother to Cersei and Jaime. He is a dwarf and hated by his father as his mother died while giving birth to him. He is a very smart and humerous character.

Petyr Baelish

Petyr Baelish is on the council of the Iron throne. He has a network of spies around Westeros and is a very sneaky character.
