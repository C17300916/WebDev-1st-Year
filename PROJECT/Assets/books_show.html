The Show

The drama and fantasy television series, written by D.B.Weiss and David Benioff is one of the most watched shows on TV ever! It's action packed plot comes to life through amazing graphics and CGI. The excellent acting compliments this and causes you to fall in love or even hate some of the characters! As a viewer you become imersed in the fantasy world that is Game of Thrones. The show is a wonderful adaptation of the books and sincerely brings them to life! The series came out in April 2011 and is awaiting it's final season set to be released in 2018 or 2019.

The Books

The fantasy novel series, written by George R.R. Martin, is also renowned for it's excellent plot and characters. While the show portrays the plot and characters excellently, th books go into even more detail! The books allow the readers mind to flow and imagine the fantasy world of Game of Thrones. Millions of readers watch both the show and read the books and love both! There are 7 books in total in the series but only 5 have been fully written and released. They include: A Game of Thrones(1996), A Clash of Kings(1998), A Storm of Swords(2000), A Feast for Crows(2005), A Dance with Dragons(2011), The Winds of Winter(yet to be released) and A Dream of Spring(yet to be released). Fans of the books eagerly await the release of the next two books.

Main Differences

Although the show is based on the novel series, there are some differences between the two. This is due to the show being incapabable of including every small plot point included in the books. Some of these differences include missing characters, small side plots and some characters appearences. The show did a great job at adapting the majorty of the books but some fans of the books complain about some major things left out. Overall the books and the show are different but both outstanding in their own right!
